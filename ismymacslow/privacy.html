Privacy Policy for isMyMacSlow

Effective date: April 21, 2026

isMyMacSlow is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that helps explain why your Mac may feel slow.

The app is designed to keep things simple and privacy-friendly.

Data Collection

isMyMacSlow does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

On-Device Processing

The app reads lightweight system signals such as CPU activity, memory pressure, storage availability, and the number of open apps. This analysis happens locally on your device.

Analytics and Tracking

isMyMacSlow does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with the developer or third parties.
