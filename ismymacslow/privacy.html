Common Questions

What does the app do?
The app shows simple system health signals such as CPU activity, memory pressure, storage availability, and app activity to help explain why the system may feel slow.

Does the app make system changes?
No. The app is informational only and does not clean, optimize, or modify your system.

Does the app require an internet connection?
No. Core analysis is performed locally on your device.
